Global discount on a sale order

You can now add a global discount on the sale order

You will find a global discount inside your sale orders.

If you add a Discount Percentage... the total will change. Here, you've got a total of 100.00 (5 x 20.00) without tax. There is a global discount of 10 %. The total without tax, is now, 100.00 - 10 % = 90.00.

Invoice your sale order

This will create at least one line of global discount. You will get one line by taxes.
